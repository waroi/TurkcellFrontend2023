Venus

STC 25

  • Size: Similar to Earth, with a diameter of 12,000 kilometers.
  • Atmosphere: Thick and composed mostly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid.
  • Surface: Covered in dense rainforests and swamps, with active volcanoes and vast deserts.
  • Resources: Abundant in exotic flora and fauna with unique medicinal properties, as well as valuable minerals and gemstones.
  • Climate: Extremely hot and inhospitable, with a runaway greenhouse effect causing temperatures to soar to scorching levels.
