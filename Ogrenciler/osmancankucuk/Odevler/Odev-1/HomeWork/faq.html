1. Follow “My Account” > “My Orders” > “Order detail”
2. Click the Generate Return Shipping Code button.
3. Select the item to be returned and the reason for the return. If you have purchased more than one product of the same product, you must also select the number of products to be returned.
4. Choose one of the suggested return methods according to the suitability of the products you want to return.
5. In case of more than one shipping option, select your cargo.
6. Take note of the return shipping code that appears on the screen. You can also find your return shipping code on the My Orders page.
7. Put the products with the same return code in the same package with the invoice and make sure that the package is intact.
8. Products with different return codes should be given in different packages, even if they are given to the same cargo.
9. According to the return method you choose, deliver your package to the cargo branch within 7 days or deliver it to the officer who will come to your address on the day of your appointment.
10. If you pass 7 days, you will need to get the return shipping code again.

1. The refund of the product you cancel may vary depending on your bank. This period may take approximately 1 week.
2. The refund process of the returned product is as follows;
• After the product reaches the seller, its compliance with the return conditions is checked within 48 hours at the latest.
• If the product complies with the return conditions, your return will be approved and your refund will be reflected on your card within
2-10 business days, depending on your bank.
• If the product does not comply with the return conditions, it will be sent back to your address.
Note:
• When a refund is made to your bank, a notification message will be sent to your membership e-mail address. The time it takes for the amount to reflect on your card/account depends on your bank.
• Refunds made to the debit card take longer to reflect on your account.
• You can view your reference number from “My Account” > “My Orders” step to check the refund from your bank.
• The cost of shopping in installments is paid to the bank at once, but your bank returns this amount to your credit card in installments.

• Check the status of your order from the "My Orders" tab in the "My Account" section on the main page.
• If your order is in the status of “Your Order Received” or “Your Order is in Process”, send us your request through the channel below. "Connect to Live Help" button on the "Help" tab in the "My Account" section
• If your order is in the status of “Delivered for Cargo”, the recipient and/or address change can only be made by the courier companies within a certain period of time. You can submit your change request in two ways.
1. By contacting the cargo company to which the cargo is sent.
2. "Connect to Live Help" button on the "Help" tab in the "My Account" section

• The products you purchase are delivered within the delivery date range specified on the product detail page. You can also find the delivery date of the products you purchased on the "My Orders" page in the "My Account" section.
• If there is a text “Delivered in 24 hours” on the product, your order will be shipped within 24 hours. These products are “FAST DELIVERY” products.
• When the order is delivered, you will be informed by the cargo company via SMS.
• After shipping, you can track the shipping stages of your order from the "Where is my Cargo?" link in the "My Orders" tab in the "My Account" section.

If you ordered the wrong product or changed your mind, you can easily cancel your products before the cargo preparations begin, as follows.
1. By following the steps “My Orders” > “Details/Order Detail” > “Cancel Order”.
2. By clicking "Connect to Live Help" from the "Help" step in the "My Account" section, selecting "Cancellation and Refund" on the page that opens. If your products have started to be prepared for shipping (your invoice has been issued) or if they have been shipped, they cannot be cancelled. In this case, you can return the product when you receive it. CLUE: Fee refunds are completed within 2 business days. You will be notified by e-mail when your return is completed and your return is made to your bank.

• If the campaign conditions continue to be met despite the cancellation or refund, the campaign will not be cancelled.
• If the products remaining in the order after cancellation or refund are not sufficient to meet the campaign conditions, the refund is made by deducting the discount amount from the campaign for the products that are canceled or returned.
