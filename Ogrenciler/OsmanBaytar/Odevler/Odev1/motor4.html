New style of the city

Feeling the minimalistic nature of the Neo Sports Café concept and Honda engineering in every detail, the CB250R is back on the road in a more environmentally friendly way with its Euro 5 compliant engine.

ABS for Safe Braking

With the CB250R, which can easily reach high speeds with its sporty character, if you need to slow down suddenly, when you press the brake, ABS is activated and you stop without locking the wheels.

Fully LED Lighting

The cult headlights of the Honda CB250R, which support the retro style, illuminate the road completely with LED technology and allow you to easily notice obstacles while driving. In addition, LED technology also contributes to the reduction of fuel consumption and emissions thanks to its low energy consumption.

Auxiliary Sliding Clutch

The CB250R makes shifting easier with an auxiliary slip clutch for extra control on city streets. In this way, smooth and comfortable driving is guaranteed at every gear change.
