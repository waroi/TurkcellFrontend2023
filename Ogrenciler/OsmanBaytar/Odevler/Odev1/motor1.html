The 30th Anniversary signature embroidered with an elegant touch on the Akrapovic silencer, which makes the Fireblade stand out not only with its performance but also with its seductive voice.

The Most Powerful Honda Ever Made

With its inline 4-cylinder engine producing 218 PS (160 kW) and 113 Nm of torque, the CBR1000RR-R Fireblade SP is the most powerful and sporty motorcycle Honda has ever produced.

In addition to its provocative power, it is one of the best in its class with a power-to-weight ratio of 1.08 PS / kg, which it achieves thanks to its body weighing only 201 kg.
