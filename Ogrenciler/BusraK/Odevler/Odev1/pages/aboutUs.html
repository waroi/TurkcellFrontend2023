Who We Are?

Genomi started its operations in 2001 as Turkey's first and only geek shop. It is the first representative of the sector, also known as "entertainment" today, in Turkey. Our store has the signature of many works in this field in Turkey. FRP games, card games such as Magic, Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, figures, sculptures and other products of the heroes of the comics and movie world first entered Turkey through Genomi. Hundreds of English and Turkish comics, manga, figures, sculptures and accessories come to our store every month and are offered to your liking.
