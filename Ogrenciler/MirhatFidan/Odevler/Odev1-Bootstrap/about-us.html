Frequently Asked Questions

You can securely pay with your credit or debit card.

All products sold are supplied from the Wunder store and delivered to cargo within 2-4 working days. An estimated arrival time of 2 business days for shipments within Istanbul and between 2 and 4 business days for shipments outside Istanbul is anticipated. The cargo company will deliver your orders to the address you specify between 09.00-18.00 on business days.

It takes an average of two weeks for the refund made to your credit card to be reflected in your bank account, depending on your bank's procedures. In addition, the process after the transfer of the refunded amount to the bank in shopping made with a credit card is not under Wunder's control, so the developments that may occur are entirely related to the bank transaction process.

Shipping costs for the products you buy are covered by us. There is a fast delivery option within the first business day for shipments within Istanbul. If the fast delivery option is preferred, an extra courier fee will be reflected on your invoice at the payment stage.

After your order cancellation request is approved by us, if the payment for your order has been received from your credit card, a refund will be initiated to your card. Please follow the return process through your bank.
