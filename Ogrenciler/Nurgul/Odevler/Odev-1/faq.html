Questions About Delivery and Cargo

Delivery times can vary depending on the shipping method and your location. Typically, standard delivery can take anywhere from 3-7 business days, while express delivery can arrive within 1-2 business days.

We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping is the most cost-effective and typically arrives within 3-7 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and typically arrives within 1-2 business days.

Shipping costs can vary depending on your location, the weight and dimensions of the package, and the shipping method you choose. You can check the shipping cost at checkout.

Questions About Returns and Exchanges

Our return policy allows for returns within 30 days of receipt of the product. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team. We'll provide you with instructions on how to return the item.

The customer is responsible for paying for return shipping unless the item is defective or the wrong item was shipped.

Once we receive the returned item, we'll process the return within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.
