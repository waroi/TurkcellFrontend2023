DM EGE KARA

D&D for me is...

a way to express my creative thoughts through fictional word of my own creation. I enjoy creating NPCs(non-player characters) that my players love or hate in character. Telling stories in an interactive way is an awesome thing for me and when you factor in that story changes by the action of players means that even one different player can make a whole different story even though both starts on the same base story design. I can take decisions made by my players and affect the world around them accordingly. I can make puzzles that forces them to think outside the box. I can create moments that make them the character they play, feel the things their characters feel in real life. As a Dungeon Master I am just the narrator of their adventures, I am their storyteller and as a storyteller there is no greater accomplishment than making those who listen to the story feel...
