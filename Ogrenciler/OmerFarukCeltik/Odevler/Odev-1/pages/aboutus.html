About US !

Foundation !

In the 1950s, our community came together as motor lovers.Since the day we've founded, we aimed to keep the cafe racer culture alive and living. we also aimed to gather our members and customers around a social club. With our social club, we modify engines, make sales and socialize. We are the best at building racing, street and dirt bikes.

Achievements

We continue to cooperate to keep the motorcycle culture alive and create a social club. We participate in international events and championships with the collaborations we have made.We aim to bring a competition culture that will continue for years to our customers and members. we also aim to gain new motor racers by enabling our members to participate in these competitions according to their wishes.
